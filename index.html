What is SkillMarket?

SkillMarket is a digital platform that connects young skilled workers with people and businesses who need their services. From tailors to tutors, from graphic designers to phone repairers – we’re helping African youth monetize their talent and reach more customers.

Key Features

  • 🔍 AI-powered service matching
  • 💬 Chat and negotiate with service providers
  • 💸 Safe payments via escrow
  • 📱 Mobile-first and offline-friendly
